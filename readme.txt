Allows you to make payments using your bank account / CCI

== Description ==

A simple payment method that does not allow you to pay commissions to a third party, such as traditional payment methods that charge commission for each sale.

### Features

* A payment method similar to bank transfer.
* With this form of payment, no commission is paid to third parties.
* This method appears in the order of the client and the trade.
* All messages are manageable.
* Add your interbank account or CCI to receive payments.

== Installation ==

### INSTALL "BBVA - WooCommerce" FROM WITHIN WORDPRESS

1. Visit the plugins page within your dashboard and select ‘Add New’;
1. Search for ‘BBVA - WooCommerce’;
1. Activate BBVA - WooCommerce from your Plugins page;

### INSTALL "BBVA - WooCommerce" MANUALLY

1. Upload the ‘BBVA - WooCommerce’ folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory;
1. Activate the "BBVA - WooCommerce" plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress;
